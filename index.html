I am a full time cyber security professional with a keen interest in cyber physical security, the capabilities as well as attack vectors, risk management and most importantly infosec community building.

I have a short quip on my LinkedIn profile, "...from policy to packet, it's all important". Although I wrote that a number of years ago I think it becomes more relevant all the time. Today our lives contain more digital elements than ever before yet breaches, service outages and critical infrastructure compromises occur regularly. Invariably this is due to a breakdown somewhere in the complex patchwork collection of software, networking or governance. I don't believe there is a single silver bullet, these are not simple problems anymore, but information security is a constantly evolving field and solutions will continue to be found if we are willing to work on them.
In addition to a new academic pursuit through the University of Essex, I do a bit of blogging and co-host a monthly podcast covering Enterprise Risk Managment topics called Caffeinated Risk. I am also active in the Calgary B|Sides conferences each year. I feel the need to pay it forward because I have benefited from so many in this field who gave much of their time and expertise, typically at little to no cost.
